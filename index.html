Provided 🎣 All gear (G-Loomis rods, Shimano reels) 💧 Bottled water 🛟 Life jackets 🧥 Rain gear (request before departure)
Fish cleaning Crew fillets the fish on the boat. A seafood shop a mile away will vacuum seal and pack it in 48-hour ice boxes to ship home or bring on your flight — though since we fish Friday morning, you'd be pushing that 48-hour window. Jeff's bringing a few coolers to fill with ice Thursday night so they're ready when we get back.

Bring a backpack for the hike + fishing day if you want snacks on the boat (you do). Bring something warm — it cools off at night and it could be very chilly on the boat in the morning since we're leaving early and the water is cold.

If you guys get sea sick or motion sickness easily, strongly consider Dramamine patches you can put on your neck. Jeff's got his sea legs, but for the land lovers (Dustin, looking at you) the last thing you want is to be blowing chunks while the rest of us catch fish. Right now it looks like a possible front with light rain could roll in toward the weekend — could cause some chop out there.
